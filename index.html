Intro

Ever feel like your week is just a series of to-dos? Endless tasks needing to be tracked and done?

Well, we feel your pain. It's why we wanted to create the world's best task manager - something that is light and fun as it is powerful and effective.

We make your daily and weekly tasks a breeze using our unique "Getter Done" design.

The Wunder Way

See our "Getter Done" design in action - you simply add your task, any notes, and a due date if you have one. It will be added to the list of your choice. Then manage and check-off your to-dos as you get them done.

Feel the satisfcation of seeing them crossed off in realtime. Using a clean and simple design, Wunderlist 2.0 keeps your focus on the task at hand.

Here's How it Works

Step 1

Simply add your task from your phone or on your desktop.

Step 2

Manage your lists, checking them off as you go.

Step 3

Get back to the things that matter, with your tasks tackled.
